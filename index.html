Organic beer

Illusion is a strawyellow all malt pilsner with low bitterness, brewed in Uganda with great care og a focus on the great taste of hop.

Non-alcoholic beer

Innocence is an alcohol free beer made with the best ingredients provided by our trusted suppliers. It aims to hit the taste of the characteristic beer taste we all know and love, without having to include the alcohol.
Innocence is bright and golden as a pilsner, with a firm white foam. The smell is sweet and delightning, the taste is fresh and light - exactly as a pilsner should be.

Our Container

We use paper containers for all of our products, shaped like an octagon, providing a great and comfortable grip for our users.
It also allows our users to personalize their own beer by filling out the "Belongs to:" field.

Custom branded beer
